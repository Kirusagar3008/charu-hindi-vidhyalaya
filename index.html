Frequently Asked Questions

What courses do you offer?

We offer Hindi Grammar Classes, Literature Studies, Exam Preparation with External Examiners, and Conversation Skills training. Both online and offline classes are available.

Do you provide online classes?

Yes, we offer flexible online Hindi classes that can be accessed from anywhere. Our online platform provides interactive sessions with experienced instructors.

What are the class timings?

Our classes are scheduled flexibly to accommodate students' needs. We offer weekday and weekend batches. Business hours are Monday to Saturday, 5:30 PM to 8:30 PM. Sunday is closed.

Do you conduct exams with external examiners?

Yes, our exam preparation includes assessments conducted by external examiners to ensure fair and unbiased evaluation of students' Hindi language skills.

What is the class size?

We maintain small class sizes to provide personalized attention to each student, ensuring better learning outcomes and individual support.

How do I enroll?

You can enroll by contacting us through the contact form, phone, or email. We also have a quick enrollment modal on our website for immediate registration.
